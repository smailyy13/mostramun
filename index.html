About MOSTRAMUN'26

MOSTRAMUN'26 is a Model United Nations conference organized under MOSTRA, bringing together students who are passionate about diplomacy, debate, leadership and global issues.

Our mission is to create an academically strong yet inclusive conference experience where delegates can develop public speaking, negotiation and policy-making skills while building meaningful connections.

Inspired by the structured content flow of international MUN conference websites, this page presents our team, committees, venue, calendar information, FAQ and application tracks in one modern experience.
