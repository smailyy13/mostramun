About

About MOSTRAMUN'26

MOSTRAMUN’26 is a Model United Nations conference organized under MOSTRA Social, bringing students together from all across the globe. As the biggest student-led MUN conference in Turkey, it represents the highest-scale platform delegates can aspire to attend.

Our mission is to deliver an academically strong and socially powerful conference that offers one of the most unforgettable MUN experiences of your life.

Backed by one of the strongest academic and public relations teams in Turkey’s MUN landscape, MOSTRAMUN sets a benchmark in both content quality and professional execution.

Throughout the conference, you will sharpen your public speaking, negotiation, and policy-making skills while building meaningful connections, forming lasting friendships, and sharing moments that will stay with you long after the final session ends.

Beyond the committee room, MOSTRAMUN’26 prepares you for your future goals by equipping you with lasting skills that shape your academic and professional journey.

MOSTRAMUN’26 is where academic excellence meets powerful social energy, and where future leaders take the floor.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the official rules of procedure (RoP) of MOSTRAMUN?

MOSTRAMUN will follow the Harvard procedure.

Is MOSTRAMUN suitable for the non-experienced?

Yes, our conference has committees suitable for first-timers. It's up to your choice but many first-timers start with GA's.

Is it mandatory to participate in all the sessions?

If you miss a total of 3 sessions at the conference, you will lose your right to receive an award; if you miss 4 sessions, you will lose your right to receive a participation certificate.

What is the dress code?

Our dress code is Western Business Attire.

Where will the conference be held?

At Yıldız Technical University, Beşiktaş Campus. You can find more information about the address at the venue page.

Will there be accommodation provided?

No, unfortunately there won't be any accommodation provided.

Are international students allowed to apply?

Yes, there is no objection to international students participating in our conference.

How can we learn more about the conference?

You can contact us via e-mail for any further questions through the e-mail address provided in the contact us section.
