Participant Commitment

You must accept the participant commitment before we can evaluate your delegate application.

MOSTRAMUN Participant Commitment Form

  1. Accuracy of Information: I affirm that all information provided in this application is complete, accurate, and truthful to the best of my knowledge, and that I have submitted it voluntarily.
  2. Application Review and Acceptance: I acknowledge and accept that the MOSTRAMUN Secretariat reserves the right to review all applications in accordance with its standard evaluation procedures. I understand that the submission of this form does not guarantee acceptance or participation.
  3. Media and Photography Consent: I grant full permission to the MOSTRAMUN Organizing Team to capture, record, reproduce, and disseminate photographs, videos, or other visual or audio materials featuring me during the conference for promotional, educational, or archival purposes.
  4. Liability and Health Disclaimer: I understand and agree that participation in MOSTRAMUN is entirely voluntary. I acknowledge that the MOSTRAMUN Organizing Team, the hosting institution, and their affiliates shall not be held responsible for any injury, illness, loss, accident, property damage, or any other form of harm or inconvenience that may occur before, during, or after the conference.
  5. Code of Conduct and Behavior: I agree to abide by the Code of Conduct, all conference rules, and the instructions issued by the MOSTRAMUN Secretariat and Organizing Team. I understand that failure to comply may result in disciplinary action, including removal from the conference without refund.
  6. Payment and Refund Policy: Upon acceptance to MOSTRAMUN, I agree to complete the required payment within three work days of receiving the confirmation of acceptance. I acknowledge that all payments are strictly non-refundable.
  7. Future Communication: I consent to the MOSTRAMUN Organizing Team contacting me in the future using the contact information provided in this form for conference-related, organizational, or official communications.
  8. Delegation Representation: If submitting on behalf of a delegation, I confirm that I have obtained the explicit consent of all listed participants regarding these terms and conditions, and I accept full responsibility for conveying them in their entirety.
Do you accept the participant commitment form?

By selecting "Yes", you confirm that you have read, understood, and agree to abide by the terms above.
